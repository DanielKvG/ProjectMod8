About

VRain is a company specialized in company safety training; first-aid and emergency action plans.

Using VR for this type of training is beneficial for the ability to simulate events at any given time, frequency and intensity. Simulating events allows users to immerse into a situation and learn the required actions hands-on. A VR-learning is an enganging and interesting way to learn first aid and emergency action plans.

VRain offers different levels of first-aid training, complete with all the needed equipment.

Packages

Comfort package

The comfort kit is advised for learning at short intervals (10 min - 45 minutes). The comfort kit requires an indoor space with electricity of at least 20m^2.

The comfort kit will allow customers to conduct a training session within their own space/facility. The customers will receive the transport/charging box with headsets and remotes. The installment team will install the headsets and take care of all the cable management. The digital help desk is available throughout the entire use duration, physical use support is included for 3 days. Customers can choose these days themselves (could be once a week for 3 weeks or 3 consecutive days after purchase for example).

Full experience package

The full experience kit is advised for more intense and complex training sessions, which can have a longer duration (>45 minutes). The full experience can be provided at location, given a 3m x 7m space for the pop-up space truck to park.

The full-experience kit is recommended for the Emergency Action Plan and for users who experience motion-sickness. The full experience kit allows customers to really immerse into their virtual environment. The pop-up space truck can be located near the office/facility and will provide the user with a headset, remote, surround sound, climatisation and a moving floor to reduce motion sickness. The installment team will install the software and remotes. The digital help desk is available throughout the entire use duration, physical use support is included for 5 days. Customers can choose these days themselves.
